Repertoire App

As a jazz musician, it's my responsibility to memorize dozens of "standard" tunes for jam sessions, which can be difficult. As a final project for my Intro to Computer Science course, a teammate and I made Repertoire App, an Android app that allows musicians to keep track of their musical repertoire. You can use it to log song information like key and tempo, keep track of practice minutes, and get suggestions for what to practice or play.
